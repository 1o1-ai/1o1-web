How It Works

  1. Connect — WebSocket (wss://), SSL-secured.
  2. Stream Audio — Raw PCM in real time to the AI engine.
  3. Inference — 7B model processes speech and generates response simultaneously.
  4. Respond — Full-duplex audio streamed back instantly.
